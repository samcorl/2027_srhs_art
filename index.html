Key Takeaways for CA Students

Best CA In-State Values

  • SJSU: ~$7,600/yr — most affordable NASAD BFA in CA
  • CSULB: ~$7,066/yr — strong LA-adjacent program
  • UC Davis: ~$14,312/yr — UC quality for fine arts
  • UCLA: ~$13,804/yr — world-class MFA pipeline
  • UCSD: ~$14,312/yr — unique computing + arts track

Best National Programs

  • RISD: #1 ranked art school, Providence RI
  • SAIC: #2 fine arts nationally (US News)
  • CalArts: #1 animation pipeline, Valencia CA
  • VCUarts: #1 public art university nationally
  • Cooper Union: Most selective + 50% scholarship

Manga / Sequential Art Programs

  • SCAD: World's 1st BFA + MFA in Sequential Art
  • SVA: Dedicated BFA in Comics (manga-inclusive)
  • MICA: Illustration BFA with Sequential Art track
  • KCAI: Illustration with Sequential Storytelling

CA Application Tips

  • UC/CSU: Apply at apply.universityofcalifornia.edu — NOT Common App
  • UC filing window: Oct 1 – Nov 30 — put it on your calendar now
  • Cal Grant: Need-based aid exclusive to CA residents
  • Private art schools (CalArts, Art Center, CCA, Otis): Common App or own portal
  • CSS Profile required for most private art school financial aid
